About

I am a PhD student in economics at the Paris School of Economics, supervised by Mathieu Parenti and Lionel Fontagné. My research studies the economic and environmental consequences of globalization, with particular focus on the design of unilateral climate policy, innovation dynamics, and international technology diffusion.

I am a research assistant at the i-MIP, working on the New Quantitative Models of International Trade programme. I am also a replicator for The Economic Journal and The Econometrics Journal.

Research

Cap-and-Trade in the Open Economy: Innovation, Exports, and the Cost of Decarbonization.
With Mathieu Parenti, Riccardo Norbiato, and Noemi Walczak. Work in progress.
